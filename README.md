🛒 Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's grocery sales performance, customer ratings, inventory distribution, and outlet operations. The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-performing product categories, and evaluate outlet-level performance across different locations and sizes.

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

- Track customer satisfaction through ratings.

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1. Sales Performance Analysis
- Tracks total sales generated across all outlets.
- Visualizes sales trends based on outlet establishment year.
- Helps identify growth patterns and business performance over time.

### 2. Product Category Analysis
- Displays sales contribution by item type.
- Highlights top-performing categories such as Fruits, Snacks, Household, Frozen Foods, and Dairy products.
- Enables better inventory planning and product management.

### 3. Fat Content Analysis
- Compares sales distribution between Low Fat and Regular products.
- Provides insights into customer preferences and purchasing behavior.

### 4. Outlet Size Analysis
- Evaluates sales generated by:
  - High-size outlets
  - Medium-size outlets
  - Small-size outlets
- Helps assess operational efficiency across outlet sizes.

### 5. Outlet Location Analysis
- Compares sales performance across:
  - Tier 1 Cities
  - Tier 2 Cities
  - Tier 3 Cities
- Identifies the most profitable geographical segments.

### 6. Customer Satisfaction Analysis
- Tracks average customer ratings.
- Measures overall customer experience and service quality.

### 7. Outlet Performance Comparison
- Provides a detailed outlet-wise comparison of:
  - Total Sales
  - Number of Items Sold
  - Average Sales
  - Customer Ratings
  - Product Visibility
